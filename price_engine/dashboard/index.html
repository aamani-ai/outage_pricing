Event catalog

An event catalog is the rule-set used to turn EAGLE-I outage snapshots into outage events before pricing.

Why it exists The raw source is a time series. The catalog decides when positive outage snapshots belong to the same event.
30 / 45 / 60 min The number is the allowed gap between snapshots before the engine splits one outage sequence into two events.
What changes Event count, duration tail, empirical S(T), annual rate, premium, and modelability can move.
What it is not Not a product version, live trigger source, regulatory filing state, or fitted duration distribution.
